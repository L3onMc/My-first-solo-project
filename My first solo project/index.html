Top three must do activities in Blantyre

Explore Chichiri Museum

Discover Malawi's rich history and culture at this iconic meseum, featuring exhibits on the country's heritage, and natural history

Visit the Blantyre Arts Festival

Experience the vibrant arts scene in Blantyre at this annual festival, showcasing local music, dance, theater, and visual arts

Hike to the top of Michiru mountain

Take in the breathing views of Blantyre and the surrounding landscape on a scenic hike up Michiru Mountain, a popular spot for nature lovers and adventres
